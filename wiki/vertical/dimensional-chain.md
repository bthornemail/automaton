# Dimensional Chain: The Complete Progression from 0D to 7D

**The Vertical Spine of Computational Topology**

---

## Overview

The dimensional chain represents the **vertical progression** through all eight dimensions (0D→1D→2D→...→7D). Each dimension builds systematically on the previous, creating a foundation-to-frontier progression.

---

## The Complete Chain

```
0D (Foundation)
  ↓
1D (Temporal)
  ↓
2D (Structural)
  ↓
3D (Algebraic)
  ↓
4D (Network)
  ↓
5D (Consensus)
  ↓
6D (Intelligence)
  ↓
7D (Quantum)
```

---

## Chain Structure

### Vertical Edges (v:*)

Each dimension connects to the next via **vertical edges**:
- **v:0D→1D**: Foundation → Temporal
- **v:1D→2D**: Temporal → Structural
- **v:2D→3D**: Structural → Algebraic
- **v:3D→4D**: Algebraic → Network
- **v:4D→5D**: Network → Consensus
- **v:5D→6D**: Consensus → Intelligence
- **v:6D→7D**: Intelligence → Quantum

### Dimensional Dependencies

Each dimension **depends on** all previous dimensions:
- **1D** depends on **0D**
- **2D** depends on **0D, 1D**
- **3D** depends on **0D, 1D, 2D**
- **4D** depends on **0D, 1D, 2D, 3D**
- **5D** depends on **0D, 1D, 2D, 3D, 4D**
- **6D** depends on **0D, 1D, 2D, 3D, 4D, 5D**
- **7D** depends on **0D, 1D, 2D, 3D, 4D, 5D, 6D**
