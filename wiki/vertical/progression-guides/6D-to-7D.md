# Transition Guide: 6D → 7D

**From Intelligence to Quantum**

---

## Overview

This guide explains the transition from **6D (Intelligence)** to **7D (Quantum)**, showing how learning systems evolve into quantum superposition and possibility exploration.

---

## What Changes?

### From 6D: Intelligence Systems

**6D provides**:
- Intelligence topology
- Learning structures
- Pattern recognition
- Meta-Log framework

**Key insight**: Intelligence and learning.

### To 7D: Quantum Systems

**7D provides**:
- Quantum topology
- Superposition structures
- Entanglement
- Quantum operations (future)

**Key insight**: Quantum and superposition.

## Common Patterns

### Pattern 1: Learning to Superposition

**6D**: System learns from experience
**7D**: System explores multiple possibilities simultaneously

**Example**:
- **6D**: Learn optimal strategy from past decisions
- **7D**: Explore all possible strategies simultaneously

### Pattern 2: Pattern to Possibility

**6D**: Recognize patterns (identify structure)
**7D**: Explore possibilities (quantum superposition)

**Example**:
- **6D**: Recognize pattern in data
- **7D**: Superpose all possible patterns

### Pattern 3: Reasoning to Entanglement

**6D**: Logical reasoning (deterministic inference)
**7D**: Quantum entanglement (correlated states)

**Example**:
- **6D**: Infer conclusion from premises
- **7D**: Entangle quantum states for correlation
