## üìö Using the Humanization Guide

### Quick Reference

**The Formula** (from HUMANIZATION_GUIDE.md):
1. Open with a Hook (question, story, intriguing statement)
2. Provide Context (Who, When, Where)
3. Explain Why It Matters (impact, significance)
4. Use Analogies (bridge known to unknown)
5. Show, Don't Just Tell (code examples, scenarios)
6. Connect to Bigger Picture (how it fits)
7. Provide Next Steps (where to go from here)

## üí° Tips for Success

### Do's ‚úÖ
- **Tell stories**: Every concept has a narrative
- **Use metaphors**: Bridge abstract to concrete
- **Show personality**: Make agents memorable
- **Ask questions**: Engage the reader
- **Provide context**: Who/What/When/Where/Why
- **Progressive complexity**: Build understanding layer by layer
- **Multiple paths**: Respect different learning styles
- **Real examples**: Show, don't just tell
- **Clear next steps**: Never leave readers hanging

### Don'ts ‚ùå
- **Don't dumb down**: Maintain accuracy
- **Don't lose technical depth**: Story supports facts
- **Don't fake enthusiasm**: Be genuine
- **Don't patronize**: Respect intelligence
- **Don't assume knowledge**: Provide context
- **Don't bury the lede**: Hook early
- **Don't overuse emojis**: Use sparingly
- **Don't forget code**: Examples are essential
- **Don't skip "why"**: Motivation matters
- **Don't leave them hanging**: Always provide next steps
