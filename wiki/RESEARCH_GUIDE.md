# Research Guide for the Computational Topology Canvas

**A Comprehensive Guide for Researchers and Thesis Writers**

## Overview

This guide provides a roadmap through the extensive research documentation created for the Computational Topology Canvas (CTC) framework. Whether you're writing a thesis, preparing a publication, or conducting research, this guide will help you navigate the documentation effectively.

---

## Research Questions Index

### Answered Research Questions

1. **Can multiple paradigms be unified?** → YES
   - See: [Research Contributions](Research_Contributions.md) - Contribution 1
   - Evidence: Working implementation with R5RS + ProLog + DataLog + RDF

2. **Can Church encoding be practical?** → YES (with caveats)
   - See: [Theoretical Foundations](Theoretical_Foundations.md) - Section 2
   - Evidence: Dimensional progression based on Church encoding

3. **How to organize agents?** → Dimensionally (0D-7D)
   - See: [Research Contributions](Research_Contributions.md) - Contribution 2
   - Evidence: 8 dimensional agents implemented

4. **Can self-modification be safe?** → YES (with snapshots)
   - See: [Research Contributions](Research_Contributions.md) - Contribution 3
   - Evidence: Automaton evolution with 100% rollback success

5. **How to coordinate logic paradigms?** → Blackboard
   - See: [Research Contributions](Research_Contributions.md) - Contribution 4
   - Evidence: Cross-paradigm queries working

### Open Research Questions

See [Future Research Directions](Future_Research_Directions.md) for comprehensive list:

1. **Formal Semantics**: What are compositional semantics of multi-paradigm programs?
2. **Category Theory**: Are dimensional transitions functors?
3. **Type Systems**: Can dimensions be encoded in dependent types?
4. **Convergence**: Under what conditions does automaton evolution converge?
5. **Scalability**: How can CTC scale to billions of triples?
6. **Neural-Symbolic**: How to integrate neural networks with logic programming?
7. **Probabilistic**: How to add uncertainty reasoning (ProbLog)?
8. **Quantum**: Can actual quantum backends be integrated?
9. **Verification**: Can evolution safety be formally verified?
10. **Usability**: What interfaces make multi-paradigm systems usable?
